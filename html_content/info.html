Welcome!

The Tiny brown bird is a blog for all things bird-related. I write about interesting bird-facts from my own and other people's research to showcase all the wonderful things we are learning about the natural world.

Why the Tiny brown bird? In "birder talk", a small brown bird is the name given to birds that are considered common, dull, and uninteresting. But should one bother to look, one would often find that the dullest-looking birds are some of the most interesting and charismatic.

This blog is celebration of the wonder of birds, especially those "boring" common ones!




I'm an evolutionary ecologist that studies birds for a living. I'm particularly interested in how birds use sound and colour to communicate.

If you have any questions about this blog or my research, please don't hesitate to contact me.
